About New Ground Australia

Why New Ground Australia Exists

In a time of increasing division and polarisation, New Ground Australia was founded to provide a voice for Australians who believe in common sense, practical solutions, and respectful dialogue. We recognised that many Australians felt their values and concerns were not being adequately represented in the public discourse.

Our movement emerged from a simple belief: that Australia's best days are ahead of us, but only if we work together with integrity, respect, and a commitment to evidence-based decision making. We exist to bridge divides, not widen them, and to offer a constructive path forward for our nation.

New Ground Australia is not about party politics or ideological purity. It's about bringing together Australians from all walks of life who share a commitment to prosperity, freedom, and the values that have made Australia a great nation.

Our Core Principles

At the heart of New Ground Australia are principles that guide everything we do:

Integrity

We believe in honest, transparent communication and holding ourselves to the highest standards of ethical conduct. Our commitment is to the truth, even when it's difficult.

Respect

We engage with differing viewpoints respectfully, recognising that good ideas can come from anywhere. We believe in civil discourse and constructive dialogue.

Practical Solutions

We focus on what works, not what sounds good. Our approach is grounded in evidence, experience, and a commitment to outcomes that benefit all Australians.

Community

We believe in the power of community and the importance of supporting one another. Strong communities are the foundation of a strong nation.

Future Focus

We make decisions with the long-term interests of Australia in mind. Our vision extends beyond the next election cycle to the next generation.

Our Vision for Australia

We envision an Australia where every citizen has the opportunity to succeed through hard work and determination. A nation where economic prosperity is shared, where individual freedoms are protected, and where our communities are strong and supportive.

We see an Australia that leads the world in innovation, education, and opportunity. A nation that respects its history while embracing its future. A country where common sense prevails, where debate is encouraged, and where diverse perspectives are valued.

Our vision includes an Australia that is economically strong, socially cohesive, and environmentally responsible. We believe in sustainable growth that benefits all Australians, not just a select few. We envision a nation where government serves the people, not the other way around.

Most importantly, we see an Australia where hope, opportunity, and the promise of a better future are accessible to all who are willing to work for it. This is the Australia we're working to build, together.
