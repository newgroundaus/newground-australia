Donate to New Ground Australia

Your contribution helps support our early-stage operational costs as we work to build a movement grounded in common sense, practical solutions, and respectful dialogue.

Purpose of Donations

Donations to New Ground Australia support our essential operational activities, including:

  • • Website hosting and maintenance
  • • Communications and outreach efforts
  • • Media and operational costs

All donations are used solely for movement-related purposes. Donations are not personal income and are held separately from any personal accounts.

Transparency & Temporary Arrangement

Current Status: New Ground Australia is currently in an early formation stage and is not yet incorporated.

Donations are held temporarily in a dedicated account that is separate from any personal accounts. This is a short-term arrangement while we work toward formal incorporation.

Once New Ground Australia is incorporated as a Pty Ltd, all funds will be transferred to a formal business bank account. We are maintaining records of all donations in good faith and will ensure a smooth transition to the formal structure.

We are committed to transparency and accountability throughout this process.

Important Disclaimers

  • Donations to New Ground Australia are not tax deductible.
  • Donations do not confer ownership, voting rights, or any personal benefit to donors.
  • All donations support the movement's activities only and are used for operational purposes as outlined above.
  • Large or anonymous donations are not encouraged at this early stage of the movement's development.

Looking Ahead: As New Ground Australia grows, we plan to establish formal structures including incorporation as a Pty Ltd, a dedicated business bank account, and additional donation methods. This page will be updated as these developments occur, and we will communicate any changes to our supporters.
