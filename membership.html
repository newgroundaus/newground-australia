Why Membership Matters

Membership supports independent media, long-term projects, and community-driven commentary in Australia. By becoming a member, you're directly contributing to the creation of thoughtful, independent content that isn't beholden to corporate interests or political agendas.

Your membership helps us maintain editorial independence, invest in quality journalism and commentary, and build a community of engaged Australians who value common sense, respectful dialogue, and evidence-based analysis.

Your Privacy Matters

We take your privacy seriously. Any information you provide will be used solely for the purposes of communicating with you about New Ground Australia and our activities. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

You can unsubscribe from our communications at any time, and you can request that we delete your information at any point. We are committed to transparency and will always be clear about how we use your data.

If you have any questions or concerns about privacy, please don't hesitate to contact us.
